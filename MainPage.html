YEC Innovative Design Competition Champions

Along with my team ENG4Life I competed in the York Engineering Competition in the Innovative Design category.

The goal of the competition was to identify an issue present in our day-to-day lives and coming up with an

innovative solution. We identified the issue of wasted energy in subway stations as trains arrive and proposed the

solution of wind turbines to regain some of this energy. For our demonstration of this concept we came first and

won the competition. I chose to showcase this work as it demonstrates how I was able to effectively communicate

our idea in a convincing and concise manner which caught the eyes of the judges allowing us to come in first place.

By presenting our idea not only using our technical knowledge but also using effective communication we were

able to go above and beyond. This ultimately allowed our topic to be understood better as we used clear and

concise language to explain an otherwise complex topic.

Mock Interview

In ENG 2003 one of our assignments was to complete a mock video interview on the Kira Talent platform. In this interview we

to be presentable so that not only do I provide well-formed answers to the questions but also looking put together and prepared.
